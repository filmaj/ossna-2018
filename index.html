Adobe's Open Source Office

Formed at the end of 2016 with the goals of improving the open source culture at Adobe. It was a grass roots type effort, with us only working on it part time. The original members were essentially volunteers as they still had day jobs. But the thing that brought them together was a shared love of open source and wanted Adobe to give back to the community. Goal: Grow open source at Adobe

Executive Support

Since we started out in more of a grass roots effort, it has been challenging to find executive support. We have had to prove ourselves constantly to grow and gain support.
